Disney World

Disney World is located in Tampa, Florida(specifically Epcot Center Dr) near Interstate 4. There are four parks and two water parks; Animal Kingdom, EPCOT, Magical Kingdom, Disney's Hollywood Studios, Disney's Typhoon Lagoon, and Disney's Blizzard Beach. You need to take Exit 65 to Animal Kingdom/Hollywood Studios/Blizzard Beach and Exit 64 to Magic Kingdom. For EPCOT/Disney's Typhoon Lagoon, take Exit 67. Inside the place, Magic Kingdom has a castle filled with princesses. Animal Kingdom, obviously, has animal attractions such as The Lion King Festival. EPCOT has a lot other rides such as the Test Track, Remy's Ratatouille Adventure, and more. Disney's Typhoon Lagoon has aggressive water rides such as Mayday Falls(Mount Mayday), Crush 'N' Gusher, and more family and children attractions. Disney's Blizzard Beach included 12 attractions including its renown Summit Plummet; a 120-foot near-vertical drop that is about 60 MPH. There are other attractions in this water park such as the Slush Gusher; a 90-foot body slide with two humps for a brief airtime. The whole park's entertainment and dining is Disney Springs, a place where you can shop, dine, play golf courses, sports complexes, and more. This place is more than 27,000 acres; The four parks and two water parks take up 1.1K acres, the rest of the land dedicated to resorts and golf courses.

Universal Orlando Resort

Universal Studios is located in Orlando, Florida, but it also has other locations in Singapore, Beijing, Japan, and California. This place has three parks and one water park; Volcano Bay, Universal Studios, Universal Islands of Adventure, and Epic Universe. Universal Studios/Universal Islands of Adventure/Volcano Bay is exit 74B on Interstate 4, and Universal Epic Universe is exit 75B on Interstate 4.
